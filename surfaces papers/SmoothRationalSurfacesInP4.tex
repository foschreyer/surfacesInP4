\subsection{Degenerate surfaces}
The plane $\PP^{2}$, $\PP^{1}\times \PP^{1}$ and the cubic surfaces $\PP^{2}(3;1^{6})$  are the smooth surfaces which do not span $\PP^{4}$. They have degree $1$, $2$ and $3$
respectively.
